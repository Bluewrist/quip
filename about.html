WHO WE ARE

Fire Quip (Pvt )Ltd is a wholly owned Zimbabwean company with its workshop and office situated at Shop A9,Sunshine Bazaar, Opposite Star FM, Simon Mazorodze Road, Harare

Our main thrust is to provide a full range of Fire Prevenon Products and services, throughout the country. We are arguably one of the best Fire companies in Zimbabwe

OUR VISION

To be the model in the provision of appropriate, affordable and reliable Fire Fighting Equipment and service.

OUR MISSION

We guarantee safety to people and property by providing appropriate ,affordable and reliable fire fighting equipment and services in Zimbabwe, through highly skilled and motivated staff, for the benefit of our customers.

OUR VALUES

We fulfill our mission guided by the following values .

CUSTOMER FOCUS

The customer is the most important person in our business. We will go an extra mile in delighting the customer with our services and products

EMPOWERMENT

We believe we will achieve more if we all exercise, initiate and use our innovation. To this end we will do everything possible to pass knowledge to our customers in line with our fundamental belief that true sustainable competitive advantage is one based on people

TEAMWORK

We will deliver greater value to all our stakeholders if we work as a system than individuals .To this end we will think and work as a team with “we” replacing “I” in all that we do in our quest to delight the customer

PROFESSIONALISM

Honesty, Intergrity, Transparency and Fairness are key attributes of our value system that will never be compromised in our dealing with our customers.

OUR STRATEGIES

Employ highly trained and motivated staff. Cultivate a culture of innovation and creativity in our staff. Adherence to sound business practices. Maintaining a high level of professionalism to ensure customer satisfaction.

STAFF

Our team is comprised of the fire technicians who all trained with City of Harare Fire Brigade Emergency services. Their experience vary from 10-20 years with some of them working as Fire Man in the department .Some of them have worked in the private sector and well versed with the expectations of the market.

AREAS COVERED IN TRAINING

  • Elements of combustion and extinction
  • Practical Firemanship
  • Fire services Equipment
  • Hydraulics and practical pump operations
  • Special appliances and introduction for B.A sets
  • Fire extinguishers, foam and foam equipment
  • Fire Brigade communication and basic fire prevention
  • Fire Prevention and Protection (Model Building By Laws )
  • First Aid
  • Theory of Fire
  • Methods of extinguishing Fires
